# Prompts de imagem — Bonfire Paradox

Prompts de geração de imagem (ChatGPT/DALL·E e afins) para a arte das criaturas.
Convenção: começar pelo **meio/técnica** (ex.: *painted fantasy illustration*), evitar palavras
como *detailed / realistic / cinematic / epic / rich*, e manter o cenário **medieval** (sem
tecnologia). Paleta-assinatura do paradoxo: **branco + violeta** com "cores fora do padrão" e o
**sub-céu** estrelado vazando pelas fendas do chão.

---

## Vorthrax, the Paradox of Dawn and Dusk

> Versão usada na key art do README (`art/Vorthrax.png`).

```text
Painted fantasy illustration. Wide distant establishing shot. SCENE: a vast corrupted landscape in
a white-and-purple color scheme, seen from far away. In the distance stands Vorthrax, the Paradox
of Dawn and Dusk — a huge ancient archdragon with two pairs of long, dragonfly-like shaped dragon
wings and four legs, shown in side profile, his head turned to look toward the LEFT of the frame,
calm and monumental, wings half-open. His body is naturally WHITE, marked with bold BLACK stripes
laid over the white, and violet-purple blotches scattered in the gaps between the stripes; the
white glows faintly and thin dark mist trails from the black markings. Glowing violet eyes; a crown
of horns. THREE MAGIC STONES float and drift around him (huge hovering boulders, NOT wheels): one
pale GOLD stone high above, one DARK stone low near the ground, one VIOLET stone off to one side,
orbiting slowly around his silhouette. ENVIRONMENT: everything is drowned in white and soft purple,
with a few off, forbidden colors that don't exist in nature. The ground is split by glowing CRACKS
and FISSURES, and inside the cracks you can see STARS — a starry night sky showing through the
broken earth, leaking violet light upward. Pale mist, a hazy white-violet sky, faint floating dust.
Medieval high-fantasy. Avoid: text, watermark, modern technology, no wheels, no rings, no normal
green landscape. Wide landscape composition.
```

**Notas de design (o que funcionou):** corpo **meia-a-meia** alvorada/crepúsculo OU **branco
listrado de preto com manchas roxas**; **asas de libélula** (dois pares) em vez de dizer "4 asas";
as três Rodas descritas como **Pedras enormes flutuantes**; estilo pintado com linha de estilo
curta rende melhor que descritor longo.
